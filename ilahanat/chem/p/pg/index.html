POLYETHYLENE GLYCOL

Polyethylene Glycol is a petroleum base-mix chemical. Abstracted from small ammount of plastic chemical that was melt and filtered. This chemical contain 3 exist-form, that are physical, gas, and also liquid. Most drug maker industries are using this chemical for its physical exist-form for make easily-swallowed drug for its oily characteristics of physical forms, for make physical drugs. Not fully dangerous type of chemicals for human body. But, it still contain small ammount of isotopes properties or burst-flameable as its properties because of its oil characteristic. The chemical is easy for solute with water. The chemical are made from [ C2nH4n+2On+1 ] as its mix-for-form formular.
